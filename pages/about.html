Vibe City is a project started by Peri and CGMT. We are interested in art, design, thought, culture, and society.
Our current interests:

  • Wong Kar Wai
  • Jiang Xueqin
  • Dieter Rams
  • Issey Miyake
  • Steve Jobs
  • Mahjong

At Vibe City, we educate ourselves and our peers at home. We bond over knowledge and beauty. We also like to make zines full of good vibes and intellectual yum-yum. So far, we have released Snapshots, ideas, quotes, transcripts from Jiang Xueqin's lectures volume 1, featuring bonus analysis and further discussion material. Volume 2 is soon to follow.

Stay tuned for updates on our Instagram page.

Vibe City works on the unceded lands of the Lekwungen peoples and the W̱SÁNEĆ peoples, as well as the Sḵwx̱wú7mesh, Tsleil-waututh, and xʷməθkʷəy̓əm peoples. We are commited to the sovereignty of all indigenous peoples on their lands.
